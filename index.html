Project Coliving Montreal

Shared housing in the heart of the Plateau designed to help you live a purpose-driven life  

ABOUT

Project Coliving is reinventing the way we live by combining functionally well designed spaces with curated events & services to facilitate a community of makers, freelancers, entrepreneurs and creators.

FULLY FURNISHED 

We have shared and single fully-furnished bedrooms. There's a huge kitchen, large dining table and a beautiful lounge where you can relax or brainstrom with the other members. You don't have to bring anything. It's that simple.

EXTRA SERVICES

Cleaning, Internet, Heating Electricity, Toilet Paper. Did I forget something ? We handle everything so you can focus on what matters the most for you. 

MONTHLY EVENTS

Community brunch, Sharing Stories, Networking, Group Sports. Our goal is to build a strong community and life-lasting friendship.

ACCESS TO A COMMUNITY

Moving to a new city can be scary and developing a good network can be long. We are here to help you develop your network and communicate with the right person in order to have what you really want.
